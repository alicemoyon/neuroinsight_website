Dr Aonghus Lawlor

medical image analysis
radiomics
AI-assisted diagnostic imaging
deep learning
computer vision techniques

Assistant Professor in School of Computer Science in UCD, Aonghus has expertise in recommender systems, machine learning and computer vision techniques. Aonghus is interested in working with fellows to apply deep learning or AI techniques to medical image analysis to improve the diagnosis and treatment of neurological or brain related conditions
